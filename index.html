Frequently Asked Questions

What is this website?

This website helps PEM & PES English students at ENSL find lessons and study materials to prepare for exams.

Who is this website for?

This website is still in the making and currently is only for PEM and PES English students (3rd year), we may add other sections and modules in the future.

Can I use this website on a laptop?

Yes! For a better, distraction-free experience, it's recommended to use it on a laptop.

Why do I sometimes see an error when opening this website?

This might happen because some Internet providers (Djezzy, Ooredoo, Mobilis..) randomly block or break access to certain websites.
